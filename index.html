Instructions

Click on a county to view it's national rankings for wildfire smoke-, heat-, cold-, drought-, and flood-related and total extreme weather event-related mortality in the "County Profile". A selection will update the "Excess Mortality Rate Ranking (National)" bar chart, and "Dollars Lost to Extreme Weather-Related Mortality" plot to state-specific values based on the state in which the county is located.

The mortality rate rankings data can be downloaded by selecting the "Download (GeoJSON)" link at the bottom of the sidebar.

Monetary Calculations

Dollars lost to extreme weather-related events were calculated using the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency's Value of Statistical Life (VSL). Losses are calculated at the state level to protect potentially identifiable health information at the county level.
